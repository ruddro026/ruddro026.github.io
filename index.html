BIOGRAPHY

Md. Husain Hamid Chowdhury Ruddro is a Biomedical Engineering graduate from Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET), Bangladesh, and currently serves as a Research Assistant at Learnify Research Lab, where his work focuses on EEG-based neural signal processing and deep learning for Brain-Computer Interfaces.

His most recent project, MSCANet, is a lightweight Multi-Scale Coupled Attention Network for motor imagery EEG decoding, achieving state-of-the-art performance on the BCIC IV-2a and IV-2b benchmark datasets. He has also developed an interpretable multi-domain machine learning framework for automated detection of Major Depressive Disorder from EEG, achieving 98.95% accuracy on the HUSM dataset.

With three IEEE conference papers published across RAAICON 2025, BECITHCON 2025, and ICRPSET 2024, Ruddro is actively building his research portfolio in preparation for PhD applications (Fall 2027) in Biomedical / Neuroengineering programs at US institutions. His broader research interests span EEG signal processing, rehabilitation engineering, neuroengineering, and biomedical image processing.

PROJECTS

MSCANet — Lightweight Multi-Scale Coupled Attention Network for MI-EEG Decoding
Learnify Research Lab · 2025 (Ongoing)
Developed a lightweight deep learning model integrating Multi-Scale Temporal CNN and a Coupled Transformer with Subject-Adaptive Batch Normalization. Achieved 83.26% / 87.32% (subject-dependent) and 60.64% / 76.14% (cross-subject) accuracy on BCIC IV-2a & IV-2b datasets. Targeting journal submission at Neurocomputing.

EEG-Based Major Depressive Disorder Detection
Learnify Research Lab · 2025 (Ongoing)
Interpretable multi-domain ML framework combining time-domain, spectral, and theta-band coherence features on the HUSM dataset (64 subjects). RF/GBC-based feature selection across six classifiers. Achieved 98.95% accuracy with 99% precision/recall/F1.
